WARTHOG

The common warthog, scientifically known as Phacochoerus africanus, is a wild pig native to sub-Saharan Africa, specifically found in grassland, savanna, and woodland habitats It is a herbivore, with a mass ranging from 60 to 150 kg for males and 45 to 75 kg for females. Warthogs are classified as Least Concern by the Encyclopedia of Life, meaning their population is stable. They have a gestation period of 152 to 183 days.
