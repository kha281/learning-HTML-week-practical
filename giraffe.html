GIRAFFE

The giraffe is alarge African hoofed mammal belonging to the genus Gifaffa .it is the tallest living terrestrial animal and the largest ruminant on Earth . Traditionally ,giraffes have been thought of as one species,Giraffa camelopardalis, with nine subspecies. Most recently reseachers proposed dividing them into four extant species due to new reseach into their mitochondrial and nuclear DNA,and individual speciescan be distinguished by their fur coat patterns.six valid extinct species of Giraffa from Africa and Pakistan are known from the fossil record.
