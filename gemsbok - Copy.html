GEMSBOK

The gemsbok, or South African oryx, is a large antelope in the genus Oryx. It is endemic to the dry and barren regions of Botswana, Namibia, South Africa and Zimbabwe, mainly inhabiting the Kalahari and Namib Deserts, areas in which it is supremely adapted for survival. Wikipedia Speed: 60 km/h (Maximum, Running) Mass: 220 – 300 kg (Male), 100 – 210 kg (Female) Conservation status: Least Concern (Population stable) Encyclopedia of Life
