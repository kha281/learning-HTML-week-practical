MONKEY TRAIL

"Monkey trails" can refer to a few different things. One common meaning in Western Canada is a beaten path where children play. In the context of play structures, "Monkey Trails" are cable structures designed to encourage children to climb, balance, and play, developing their physical and mental skills. Additionally, "Monkey Trails" is the name of a travel company specializing in African safaris.
